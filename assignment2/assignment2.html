ASSIGNMENT 02

FACE GENERATOR

A02

Project Reflection

My character is inspired by the Japanese cartoon Anpanman. When I read the requirement about using random values, I decided to make the eyes change color, as if he were at a party and the lights were constantly shifting.

During the process, I realized that the size and position of each part of the face needed to be relative to the overall face, not fixed values. If I used absolute coordinates, then when the face became very small, the facial features would drift outside the edges of the head or lose their proportions.

By switching everything to relative values, the face can stretch or shrink while keeping its structure. The cheeks, eyes, nose, and mouth all follow the same logic, so the drawing still feels intentional even when the user drags the mouse to create extreme sizes.
