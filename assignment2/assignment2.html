Project Description

My figure is inspired by Japanese cartoon, Anpanman. When I check the requirement about the random value. I think about making the eye changing color, like he is having a party.

During the process, I found out that the value and coordination of each part of the face has to be a relative value to the whole face, otherwise, if the position is a fixed number, when the face is small enough, the facial parts will go out of the boundary.
