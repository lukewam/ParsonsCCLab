ARTIST NOTE

LW-01

Hello.

I studied Computer Science at UC San Diego, where I built a strong foundation in computational thinking and technical problem-solving. Over time, I realized that what moved me most was not just how systems work, but how people experience them.

That realization led me to the Design and Technology MFA program at Parsons, where I focus on interaction design, game design, and experimental digital media. I’m interested in interfaces that feel slightly off, slightly eerie — somewhere between tool, toy, and scene.

My goal is to create work that feels like an encounter: quiet, emotional, and a little cinematic. I want my designs to slip past pure function and sit closer to art — something you return to, rather than just use once.
