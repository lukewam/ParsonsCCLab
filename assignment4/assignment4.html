ASSIGNMENT 04

EXQUISITE CORPSE

A04

Project Reflection

Partners: Valentine Ye, Iana Tchantchaleishvili

This project follows the traditional “Exquisite Corpse” method, where each collaborator creates one section of a character without seeing what the others are drawing. The goal is to embrace randomness, surprise, and the unexpected combinations that appear when different styles collide.

We divided the character into parts — head, body, arms, skirt, boots — and assigned them separately. Because none of us looked at the others’ work until the end, the final figure becomes a playful hybrid: a panda head, a heart-shaped body, expressive arms, a geometric skirt, and stylized boots.

What I enjoyed most was how coding these shapes feels similar to collage: each piece is constructed through coordinates, curves, and color choices, but the personality comes from their unexpected assembly. The final result is quirky, surreal, and fun — exactly what an Exquisite Corpse should be.
